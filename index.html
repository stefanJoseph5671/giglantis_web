Why Giglantis?
Built for the Decentralized Finance (DeFi), dedicated to provide a trustless marketplace for the freelancers Decentralized freelancing is a huge use case and plays a vital role for blockchain technology and decentralized finance. Freelancing has been notoriously plagued by centralized intermediaries, and a trustless architecture like Giglantis could greatly increase trust in a network and solve numerous inefficiencies. Core Features and Functions
No Centralization, Anywhere System is designed to be completely trustless and decentralized. Buyers and freelancers can make deals directly. Intermediary intervention is minimum. Learn More

GUI Layer (Web & Mobile) Giglantis web and mobile interface will be interactive and easy to use. Anyone can register/login to the platform and conduct the business seamlessly. The user profiles and stats are provided to gauge their performance. Furthermore, the UI also serves as a wallet, payment system, staking and governance interface. Learn More
Protocol Layer Giglantis Protocol is an innovative way of handling various tasks in the platform itself. Buyer can post a task, seller can post their field of expertise and our inbuilt AI does its magic and try to match them accordingly. The protocol layer mainly consists with the Payment processor, Staking platform, Liquidity provision, Decentralized Governance and NFT Marketplace. Learn More
Blockchain Nucleus Layer Giglantis Nucleus is literally the brain of operations. It consists with an array of smart contracts and collective attributes of the protocol which play the same role as backend of the centralized counterparts.
