User Layer
Giglantis core user base comprise predominantly with buyers (Users who are buying or intend to buy a service via Giglantis DAPP) and service providers (freelancers). Aside from that, there are other user categories such as staking participants, governance participants etc…
GUI Layer (Web and Mobile)
Giglantis web and mobile interface will be interactive and easy to use. Anyone can register/login to the platform and conduct the business seamlessly. The user profiles and stats are provided to gauge their performance. Furthermore, the UI also serves as a wallet, payment system, staking and governance interface.
Protocol Layer
Giglantis Protocol is an innovative way of handling various tasks in the platform itself. Buyer can post a task, seller can post their field of expertise and our inbuilt AI does its magic and try to match them accordingly. The protocol layer mainly consists with the Payment processor, Staking platform, Liquidity provision, Decentralized Governance and NFT Marketplace.
Blockchain Nucleus Layer
Giglantis Nucleus is literally the brain of operations. It consists with an array of smart contracts and collective attributes of the protocol which play the same role as backend of the centralized counterparts. For instance, Order Storage, Swap, Bank, Pool, Oracle, etc… contracts. All the data flowing through the backend is open to public (as it should be) and verifiable.
